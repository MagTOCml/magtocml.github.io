The MagTOCml Project

The MagTOCml project is part of the FactMiners.org Digital Humanities reseach mission. Started by Citizen Scientists Jim Salmons and Timlynn Babitsky, we are an unfunded and independent group dedicated to the preservation and exploration of print era magazines with a focus on the digital collections at the Internet Archive.

Our primary goal is the development of the MAGAZINEgts ground truth storage format and its reference implementation on the Apple Computer focused edition of Softalk magazine (1980-84).

We will explain more about us and our project in the days ahead. For now, we're just focused on getting the project going.
